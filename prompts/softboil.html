Method



  1. Bring a saucepan (large enough to hold the
    eggs in a single layer) of water to a boil over medium-high heat.

  2. Using a slotted spoon, gently lower the cold eggs into the water one at a time.

  3. Cook for exactly 6½ minutes, adjusting the heat as necessary to maintain a gentle boil.

  4. Meanwhile, fill a bowl with cold water and some ice cubes. When the eggs are done cooking, transfer them to the water and chill until just slightly warm, about 2 minutes.

Soft Boiled Eggs



Serves: 4
Time: Cooks in 6½ minutes
